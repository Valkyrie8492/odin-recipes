Karaage

An izakaya staple: crispy fried chicken with lots of flavor

Any solid ramen shop or izakaya is almost guarateed to have this treat right near the top of the starters

With a little bit of prep you can easy make this as a meal or snackable for guests

Best served with a cheap, cold beer and several friends

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Soy sauce or shoyu
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Sesame oil
  • Mirin
  • Flour
  • Cornstarch

Directions

  1. Slice the chicken thighs into bite size pieces and place them into a large ziploc bag to marinate along with the soy, garlic, mirin, ginger, and sesame oil
  2. After an hour, prepare the dredge by mixing the cornstarch and flour, then toss the chicken in it to coat
  3. Bring fry oil to 325 and fry the chicken to a crispy finish. Assuming bite size pieces, it should take ~5 minutes to cook through
  4. Drain the chick on a paper towel or wire rack, season with salt while hot, and serve with a slice of lemon
